abstract: "Homeostasis is kind of force that makes living organism to live. In this study, we suggest an integral equation that models homeostasis in living organism. We also showed that various situations can be modeled by homeostasis, and give mathematical interpretation of mechanism of living organism. With our proposed integral equation, one can handle homeostasis quantitatively, and this approach is expected to unveil various hidden properties of living organism."
